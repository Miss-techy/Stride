About Us

Stride is a manufacturing and e-commerce company. We are into footwear of all kinds, socks and bags. What are you looking for? A backpack for school? A pair of socks to wear to work? Sneakers for jogging? A pair of georgeous high heels for that dinner party? A durable travel bag for a weekend trip? No worries, we have your back. Our products are made from materials like bamboo, cork ,pinatex, raffia, organic cotton and jute. Our raw materials are bio-degradable because taking care of the environment and eco system matters to us. Yes, no animals are killed for leather production. Thank you for being an eco fashionable consumer.
